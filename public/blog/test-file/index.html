Welcome to my blog

Posted on Mar 13, 2025

Are blogs still a thing in 2025? Does anyone still read them? I have admittedly nebulous hopes for this thing at the moment, but I intend for it to be a place to write posts dedicated to music. Things I notice in musical works, lessons I learn in composition, software tips and tutorials… perhaps one day I will implement more of a structure but for now I intend to post just something once per week. It’s my first time doing anything like this, some 15 years past the prime of The Blog.

For today, I don’t have much. The piece that made me fall head over heels in love with orchestra music was Mahler’s 9th symphony. And in the past week I’ve been listening non-stop to two pieces: Brahms’ 4th symphony and Bruckner’s unfinished 9th symphony. The Brahms is delightful and cinematic in a way I felt was quite ahead of his time. People often refer to Bruckner with those descriptors: “epic”, “cinematic”. Which he absolutely can be at times with that huge thunderous brass of his.
